About NEST Hackathons

NEST Hackathons serve as innovation accelerators, bringing together bright minds to tackle real-world challenges unique to the Northeast region. These intensive, time-bound events foster collaboration between students, startups, researchers, and rural innovators.

Our hackathons focus on solving regional challenges—from sustainable agriculture and bamboo-based industries to AI-driven solutions and eco-friendly materials. By aligning with NEST's four official technology verticals, we ensure solutions that are both innovative and locally relevant.

We actively encourage participation from rural youth, early-stage startups, and academic researchers, creating an inclusive ecosystem where diverse perspectives drive breakthrough innovations.

Key Highlights

  • Aligned with NEST's four technology verticals
  • Regional challenge-focused problem statements
  • Mentorship from IIT Guwahati faculty & industry experts
  • Government-backed initiative with institutional support
  • Pathway to incubation and funding opportunities
