About NEST Hackathons

Innovation Accelerators for Northeast India

NEST Hackathons serve as innovation accelerators, bringing together bright minds to tackle real-world challenges unique to the Northeast region.

These intensive, time-bound events foster collaboration between students, startups, researchers, and rural innovators. Our hackathons focus on solving regional challenges—from sustainable agriculture and bamboo-based industries to AI-driven solutions and eco-friendly materials.

By aligning with NEST's four official technology verticals, we ensure solutions that are both innovative and locally relevant. We actively encourage participation from rural youth, early-stage startups, and academic researchers, creating an inclusive ecosystem where diverse perspectives drive breakthrough innovations.

Innovation Focus

Thematic Focus Areas

Our hackathons align with NEST's official technology verticals, driving innovation across critical domains.

🌱 Grassroot Technology Innovation

Rural engineering solutions, community-driven innovation, and locally adaptable technologies for Northeast communities.

⚡ AI & Semiconductor Systems

Emerging computing systems, automation, intelligent applications, and semiconductor design for next-gen solutions.

🎋 Bamboo-Based Technology

Sustainable materials, value-added manufacturing, and bamboo entrepreneurship aligned with regional resources.

♻️ Eco-friendly Plastics

Biodegradable alternatives, green materials innovation, and sustainable packaging solutions for a cleaner future.
