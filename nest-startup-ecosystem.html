About Startup Support

NEST operates on a Hub-and-Spoke model, with IIT Guwahati serving as the central Innovation Hub for the Northeast region. This structure enables coordinated technology transfer, commercialization pathways, and incubation support across the eight states.

The Hub facilitates connections between academic research and industry needs, creating clear pathways for technology commercialization. Startups and innovators gain access to institutional resources, lab facilities, and expert mentorship to accelerate their journey from idea to market.

Through structured incubation programs and mentorship support, NEST helps early-stage ventures navigate regulatory frameworks, refine business models, and secure market linkages—building a robust entrepreneurship ecosystem rooted in regional strengths.
