Who We Are

About the NEST Cluster

The North Eastern Science & Technology (NEST) Cluster is a flagship innovation hub established at IIT Guwahati with an investment of ₹22.98 crore, supported by the Ministry of Development of North Eastern Region (DoNER), Government of India.

NEST serves as the nerve centre of the Northeast's innovation ecosystem — a place where centuries of indigenous wisdom meet the rigour of modern science. It translates local knowledge, traditional crafts, and regional resources into scalable, globally competitive technologies.

By uniting academia, industry, government, and grassroots communities, NEST builds a sustainable innovation future aligned with India's Act East Policy — spanning all seven sister states of Northeast India.

Hub & Spoke Model

Women Empowerment & Grassroots Training

Rural women from Kamrup district completed training in biodegradable toy manufacturing using Industry 4.0 technologies. As a symbol of ownership, these women designed the official NEST Cluster logo, unveiled at the inauguration ceremony.

NE-SPARKS & Youth Exchange

Under DoNER's NE-SPARKS and Ashtalakshmi Darshan programmes, 3,200 students from across India will visit the Northeast. Simultaneously, 800 students from NE states gain hands-on scientific experience at ISRO and premier national institutions.
