About

The North Eastern Science & Technology (NEST) Cluster is a strategic innovation initiative established at IIT Guwahati.
Formally inaugurated on November 2, 2025, by Shri Jyotiraditya M. Scindia, Hon'ble Minister for Development of North Eastern Region (DONER), Government of India.
The cluster serves as a regional "nerve center" to accelerate research, skill development, and industrial growth across the eight northeastern states.
