Disaster Categories

Major Disaster Types

🌊 Flood

Severe annual flooding especially in Assam and Tripura.

Before: Store water & essentials

During: Move to higher ground

After: Avoid contaminated water

🌋 Earthquake

Entire Northeast falls under Seismic Zones IV and V.

During: Drop, Cover & Hold

⛰️ Landslide

Frequent in hilly states like Arunachal, Mizoram & Meghalaya.

Warning Signs: Cracks, tilting trees

🌀 Cyclone & Storm

Impact mainly in Tripura and lower Assam regions.

Before: Secure loose objects
