Our Verticals

Four Focused Innovation Verticals for the North Eastern Region

Grassroot Tech AI & Semiconductor
Vertical 01

Innovation Hub on Grassroot Technologies

Scientific validation and advancement of traditional and rural technologies

🌱 Flood Resilient Construction

Developing and validating construction methods suitable for flood-prone conditions in the Northeast.

🏠 Sustainable Rural Housing

Creating cost-effective, climate-responsive housing solutions using local and natural materials.

💧 Smart Irrigation Systems

Strengthening traditional water management practices with sensor-supported systems.

🌾 Village Level Processing

Establishing rural-scale solutions for food processing, agro-product value addition.

Vertical 02

Technology Hub for Semiconductor and Artificial Intelligence

Advancing emerging technologies aligned with national priorities

🔬 Semiconductor Design

Providing training and research in chip design, VLSI systems, and semiconductor simulation tools.

🤖 AI-Enabled Automation

Building AI-based solutions for regional sectors including agriculture, healthcare, and disaster response.

🧠 Machine Learning

Conducted applied machine learning research tailored to the unique environments of the Northeast.

📡 Embedded Systems

Developing IoT and embedded system solutions suitable for rural and semi-urban deployment.

Vertical 03

Centre of Excellence for Bamboo Based Technology

Leveraging the Northeast ecological advantage in bamboo resources

🎋 Engineered Bamboo

Research and development of laminated bamboo composite panels and structural bamboo products.

🏗️ Sustainable Construction

Developing bamboo-based alternatives to conventional materials for resilient structures.

🪑 Industrial Products

Designing furniture, textiles, packaging materials from processed bamboo.

✅ Standardization

Establishing quality standards and certification for national and international markets.

Vertical 04

Skill Development and Innovation Centre for Biodegradable and Eco Friendly Plastics

Promoting circular economy practices and sustainable material innovation

🧪 Biopolymer Research

Developing and testing bio-based polymers derived from regional agricultural feedstocks.

📦 Sustainable Packaging

Creating commercially viable, environmentally responsible alternatives to single-use plastic.

♻️ Waste-to-Resource

Converting agricultural and organic waste into raw material for sustainable production.

🌿 Green Manufacturing

Vocational and technical training programs in sustainable materials production.
